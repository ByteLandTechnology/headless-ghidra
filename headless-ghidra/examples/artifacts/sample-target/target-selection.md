## Rules

- `Selection Reason`, `Frontier Reason`, `Question To Answer`, and
  `Tie-Break Rationale` are the minimum review fields for an automatic default
  target.
- `Secondary Metrics` may remain visible in the main surface, but `Metrics Note`
  must keep them explicitly secondary.
- `Deviation Reason` and `Deviation Risk` are required only when the workflow
  records a documented deviation.
- Before a row moves from `ready` to `approved`, record the replacement
  boundary and fallback strategy for that step in `reconstruction-log.md` and
  `comparison-command-log.md`.
- Runtime generation should write to a workspace copy under
  `.work/ghidra-artifacts/<target-id>/target-selection.md`.
